# Hormozi + Martel Frameworks Reference

Condensed from Alex Hormozi (*$100M Offers*, *$100M Leads*) and Dan Martel (*Buy Back Your Time*).
Used by the lead-strategy skill to build actionable go-to-market plans.

---

## PART 1: HORMOZI FRAMEWORKS

### 1. The Value Equation

> Value = (Dream Outcome x Perceived Likelihood of Achievement) / (Time Delay x Effort & Sacrifice)

| Lever | Direction | How |
|-------|-----------|-----|
| Dream Outcome | Increase | Make the end state vivid and specific. "Get 3 clients" is weak. "Replace your salary in 90 days" is strong. |
| Perceived Likelihood | Increase | Add proof (case studies, testimonials, credentials). Add a guarantee. Show the mechanism. |
| Time Delay | Decrease | How fast do they see results? Compress the timeline or show quick wins. |
| Effort & Sacrifice | Decrease | What do they have to do vs. what do you handle? "Done for you" beats "figure it out yourself." |

### 2. The Grand Slam Offer

An offer no one can refuse because it solves the whole problem, not part of it.

**Structure:**
1. **Niche down** — pick a specific person with a specific problem
2. **Name the dream outcome** — use their words, not yours
3. **Stack the value** — list every piece of the solution
4. **Remove obstacles** — for each step they'd struggle with, show how you handle it
5. **Reverse the risk** — guarantee that removes their fear of loss

**Formula:**
> "I help [specific person] get [dream outcome] in [timeframe] without [main obstacle]."

### 3. The Four Core Lead Gen Channels

Hormozi's framework: there are only 4 ways to get customers.

| Channel | Speed | Cost | Scalability |
|---------|-------|------|-------------|
| **Warm Outreach** | Fast | Free | Limited (your network) |
| **Cold Outreach** | Fast | Low | High |
| **Content (free)** | Slow | Free | Very High |
| **Paid Ads** | Fast | High | Very High |

**Rule:** Start with warm outreach (fastest to revenue), add cold outreach for scale, build content for inbound, add paid ads when you have a proven offer.

### 4. Lead Magnets ($100M Leads)

A lead magnet solves ONE specific problem completely and for free.

**7 characteristics of a great lead magnet:**
1. Solves a narrow, specific problem (not "get better at business")
2. Reveals a problem they didn't know they had
3. Delivers immediate value (under 10 minutes to consume)
4. Makes them realize they need more help (from you)
5. High perceived value, low actual cost to deliver
6. Related directly to your core offer
7. Has a compelling name (not "Free PDF")

**Formats:** Checklist, template, calculator, mini-course, audit, assessment, tool.

### 5. Lead Nurture Sequence

After someone engages with your lead magnet:

1. **Day 0:** Deliver the lead magnet + welcome message
2. **Day 1:** Share a quick win story (proof it works)
3. **Day 3:** Teach something new that builds on the lead magnet
4. **Day 5:** Share a case study with specific numbers
5. **Day 7:** Make the offer with a clear CTA

### 6. Cold Outreach Framework

**The 3 rules:**
1. Personalize the first line (reference something specific about them)
2. Lead with value or curiosity, not a pitch
3. One ask per message: "Are you open to a conversation?" not "Buy my thing"

**Cold DM Template:**
```
Hey [Name] — saw your [specific thing]. [Genuine compliment or observation].

Quick question: are you currently [problem your offer solves]?

I work with [ICP description] and we've been getting [specific result] in [timeframe].

Would it make sense to have a quick chat to see if I could help?
```

**Cold Email Template:**
```
Subject: [Specific result] for [their company/niche]

Hi [Name],

I noticed [something specific about their business].

We help [ICP] achieve [dream outcome] without [main obstacle].

Recently helped [similar company/person] get [specific result] in [timeframe].

Would a 15-minute call make sense this week?

[Your name]
```

**Follow-up cadence:**
- Day 1: Initial message
- Day 3: Value bump (share a relevant resource)
- Day 7: Social proof (share a result)
- Day 14: Different angle / new hook
- Day 21: Breakup message ("Last note from me...")

### 7. Discovery Call Framework

**Phase 1 — Qualify (5 min)**
- "Tell me about what you're working on."
- "What's the main thing you're trying to solve?"

**Phase 2 — Connect (10 min)**
- "How long has this been a problem?"
- "What have you tried already?"
- "What would it mean if you solved this in 90 days?"

**Phase 3 — Present (10 min)**
- Walk through the offer, tie each piece to what they said
- Show relevant proof

**Phase 4 — Close (10 min)**
- "Does this feel like the right fit?"
- Handle objections directly

### 8. Key Metrics to Track

| Metric | Target | Fix If Low |
|--------|--------|-----------|
| Outreach sent / week | 20+ | Block dedicated time |
| Reply rate | >10% | Improve personalization |
| Call booking rate | >50% of replies | Improve follow-up sequence |
| Call close rate | >25% | Improve discovery call / offer |
| Client LTV | [your target] | Add upsells, retainers, referrals |

## PART 2: DAN MARTEL FRAMEWORKS

### 9. Buy Back Your Time

**Core principle:** Your time has a dollar value. Anything below that rate should be delegated, automated, or eliminated.

**The Buyback Rate:**
> Take your annual revenue goal, divide by 2,000 hours = your effective hourly rate.
> Anything you can pay someone (or an AI) less than that rate to do: delegate it.

**Time Audit:**
1. Track everything you do for one week
2. Categorize: $10/hr tasks, $100/hr tasks, $1000/hr tasks, $10,000/hr tasks
3. Systematically eliminate or delegate everything below your target rate
4. Only YOU should do: vision, strategy, key relationships, high-leverage sales

### 10. The Replacement Ladder

The order in which to buy back your time:

| Stage | What to Delegate | Why |
|-------|-----------------|-----|
| 1. Admin | Email, scheduling, data entry, filing | Lowest value, easiest to hand off |
| 2. Delivery | Fulfillment, customer service, operations | Frees you from being the bottleneck |
| 3. Marketing | Content creation, social media, lead gen | Systems run without you |
| 4. Sales | Discovery calls, follow-ups, closing | You focus only on vision + strategy |

**For AI assistants:** Position your product on this ladder. Which rung does it handle? The higher up the ladder, the more valuable the offer.

### 11. The DRIP Matrix

A system for turning your expertise into scalable assets:

| Step | Action | Output |
|------|--------|--------|
| **D**o | Do the task yourself first | Personal experience |
| **R**ecord | Record yourself doing it (video, SOP, template) | Reusable asset |
| **I**mprove | Refine the process based on feedback | Better system |
| **P**romote | Turn it into content, a tool, or a training | Lead magnet / marketing asset |

### 12. Dream 100 (Martel's Adaptation)

Identify 100 people or companies who already have your ideal customers' attention.

**Categories:**
1. **Podcasters** in your niche (guest appearances)
2. **Newsletter writers** your ICP reads
3. **Community leaders** (Slack groups, Facebook groups, forums)
4. **Complementary service providers** (they serve your ICP but don't compete)
5. **Influencers/thought leaders** your ICP follows

**Engagement strategy:**
1. Follow, engage, and add value for 30 days before asking anything
2. Share their content with genuine commentary
3. Offer to collaborate: guest post, podcast swap, joint workshop
4. Ask for introductions only after you've provided value first

### 13. The Perfect Week

Martel's time-blocking framework for entrepreneurs:

| Day | Focus | Activities |
|-----|-------|-----------|
| Monday | Admin + Planning | Review metrics, plan the week, clear inbox |
| Tuesday | Outreach + Sales | Cold outreach, follow-ups, discovery calls |
| Wednesday | Content + Marketing | Create content, engage Dream 100, nurture leads |
| Thursday | Outreach + Sales | Cold outreach, follow-ups, discovery calls |
| Friday | Strategy + Learning | Review what worked, improve systems, learn |

**Rules:**
- Batch similar activities on the same day
- Protect your high-value blocks (no meetings during deep work)
- Every day starts with 90 min of your highest-leverage activity

## PART 3: COMBINED STRATEGY FRAMEWORK

### The 90-Day Lead Gen Sprint

**Week 1-2: Foundation**
- Define ICP with extreme specificity
- Craft your Grand Slam Offer using the Value Equation
- Build one lead magnet
- Set up tracking (spreadsheet or CRM)
- Build your Dream 100 list

**Week 3-4: Launch Outreach**
- Start warm outreach (personal network, existing contacts)
- Send first batch of cold outreach (20/week)
- Begin engaging Dream 100 on social
- Publish first 3 pieces of content

**Week 5-8: Scale**
- Increase cold outreach to 40/week
- Run lead nurture sequences
- Book discovery calls, refine your pitch
- Double down on what's working, cut what isn't
- First referral asks

**Week 9-12: Optimize**
- Review all metrics
- A/B test outreach messages
- Tighten discovery call script
- Build case studies from first clients
- Plan paid ads if organic is converting
